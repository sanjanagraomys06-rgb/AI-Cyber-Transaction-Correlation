# SentinelAI System Architecture

The SentinelAI platform consists of the following major components:

1. Cybersecurity Telemetry Collection
2. Banking Transaction Monitoring
3. AI Correlation Engine
4. Fraud Detection Module
5. Quantum Risk Monitoring
6. Explainable AI Engine
7. Risk Scoring Engine
8. Dashboard & Alert Management

The architecture is designed for scalability, real-time processing, and explainable decision-making.
